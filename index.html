Tumble was an old cat who lived beneath my house. He had thirty-two scars each a different color. His active days may long be gone and he is aged and saged, and his three little kittens look up to him as a leader to follow or a figure to admire, but he may still on boresome nights be seen chasing the odd moth.

A wizard named Petunius knocked on my door at midday. She had a few scrolls, at least seven, and an owl on her shoulder. And a long pointy hat, so long that it's point and her feet could not be viewed together with a single glance.
